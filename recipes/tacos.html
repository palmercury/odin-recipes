Delicious Turkey Tacos

Description

Turkey tacos are a hit for any night of the week. This taco meat can be used on top of a variety of dishes, not limited to potatoes, rice or even salad! Not only is this recipe quick and easy to make, it will become a favorite dish in your household! The ground turkey is a bit healthier, but this recipe can also use ground beef, pork or chicken with similar results. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Tbsp of canola oil
  • 1 lb of ground turkey
  • 1 yellow onion (chopped)
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 Tbsp of chili powder
  • 2 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 10 oz can of tomatoes with chilies (undrained)
  • Optional: 1 sequeeze of fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil, ground turkey meat, onion, and garlic to skillet. Stir and cook until the meat has fully browned.
  2. Add chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt and the can of tomato and chilies. Stir to combine.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and continue to cook for 1-2 minutes until the meat mixture is heated through.
  4. Optional:Squeeze fresh lime (about 1 tsp) over the dish. Serve and enjoy!
