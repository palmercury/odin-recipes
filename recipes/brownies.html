Chocolate Brownies

Description

This is a brownie recipe that you'll never forget! They quick and easy to make, with just a few ingredients. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vinalla extract
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cute of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tablesppons of butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cut of butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup of cocoa, 1/2 cup of flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in a preheated over for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  4. To make the frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons of softened butter, 3 tablespoons of cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost the brownies while they are still warm.
