Eggroll in a Bowl

Description

If you're craving Chinese food without all the fat, eggroll in a bowl is perfect for you! Not only is it healthier, but it's quick and easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground turkey (you can substitute ground chicken, beef or pork)
  • 1 sweet onion (diced)
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons of fresh garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 (16 oz) bag of coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 cup of matchstick carrots
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce (strongly recommended)

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground meat and cook. Stir the meat until it is no longer pink. Drain and return meat to skillet.
  2. Add diced onion, sesame oil, and rice vinegar to the skillet. Cook and stir until the onion is tender.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, hoisin sauce (optional), coleslaaw mix, and carrots to the skillet. Cook and stir for about 5-7 minutes, or until the cabbage is wilted.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat. Stir in green onions and season with salt and pepper.
